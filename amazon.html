Echo

The Echo is the baseline product in Amazon's lineup. Coming in five different colours to match any room, a reasonable price tag, and a great speaker. Using this device is very enjoyable and should suit the needs of most people looking for a smart assistant. You can combine this with many smart home devices, some even in package deals when purchasing it on Amazon's website.

Echo Plus

The Echo Plus is the large variation in the Echo lineup. The Echo Plus has a fantastic speaker and shares the same features as the other Echo devices. If you can afford the more premium price tag, this device can easily replace or work with existing speakers in your house. The Echo Plus can be paired with Amazon's Echo Sub for a mind blowing speaker system. This Echo device comes with a philips hue bulb included in the price tag (special offer which is often available).

Our Rating

We gave the Echo Plus this rating because of it's speaker quality and potential combination with other speakers to add a smart assistant and new sound system to any living environment.
