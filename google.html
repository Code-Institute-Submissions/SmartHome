Google Home Mini

As the name states, this is the small version of the Google Home. Don't be fooled by its size, this Google Home Mini packs a punch with it's sound quality and search ability. This device directly competes with Amazon's Echo Dot where it wins in terms of search ability and conversation ability but loses when it comes to smart home compatibility .

Our Rating

We gave the Google Home Mini this rating because it provides great value for money and although it's only a newly introduced device, it can still compete well with Amazon's more experienced Echo Dot.

Google Home

This device brings the same great features as it's mini version but carries a more attractive design, a better speaker, and touch control on the top. The Google Home looks and feels premium with a neutral design to suit most rooms. When paired with a Google Chromecast, this device can provide voice controlled entertainment with human-like responses and conversation.

Our Rating

We gave the Google Home this rating because it's a great device but we feel like the improvements from the Home Mini aren't significant enough to charge €90 more.
