The Beginning of Your Smart Life

Everyday Uses

  • Depending on which assistant you choose, you gain many new handy features that you can use in your everyday life.

  • Features include; setting timers, translating languages, making a shopping list, playing music, and taking phone calls.

  • There are many more features to choose from depending on which assistant you get. You can even set up routines so that your assistant tells you your schedule for the day, what the weather will be like and how long it will take you to get to work.

Combining Tech

  • To get the most out of your smart home assistant, you can pair it with accessories and your phone.

  • The most common accessories include; smart lights, temperature control, smart plugs, TV's, and even doorbells with a camera to see who's knocking.

  • Once you set up your assistant and learn how it works, you can have the ability to combine it with almost any piece of tech in your house. You could even wake up to a coffee if you ask! In order to do this, there are apps you can get like 'IFTTT' where the community programs your assistant to do whatever it is you need done.
